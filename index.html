About this page

The goal of this page is to demonstrate basic use of heading and paragraph tags in HTML.

What Are Heading Tags?

Heading tags ranging from <h1> to <h6>, define headings of varying importance or size.

What Are Paragraph Tags?

The <p> tag is used to define a paragraph of text.
